Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Greenok provides automation, chatbot, CRM, messaging, and integration services for businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and delete information when users interact with our services, websites, chatbots, automations, or connected social media accounts.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Basic profile information provided by platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, or other messaging channels.
  • Names, usernames, profile IDs, page IDs, conversation IDs, and similar identifiers.
  • Messages, inquiries, images, attachments, and other content sent to our connected accounts or chatbots.
  • Contact details voluntarily provided by users, such as phone number, email address, or delivery/contact information.
  • Technical logs needed to operate, secure, debug, and improve our services.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to user messages and customer inquiries.
  • Provide chatbot, automation, CRM, and customer support functionality.
  • Route conversations to human operators when needed.
  • Improve service quality, reliability, analytics, and troubleshooting.
  • Comply with legal, security, and platform requirements.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only with service providers, platforms, hosting providers, CRM systems, automation tools, or messaging services necessary to operate our services.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide our services, maintain business records, resolve issues, comply with legal obligations, or improve our systems.

User Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their personal data at any time by emailing us at greenokindo@gmail.com. Please include your name, contact details, and the Facebook or Instagram username/profile related to your request.

We will review and process valid deletion requests within a reasonable time, unless we are required to keep certain information for legal, security, fraud prevention, or legitimate business reasons.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: greenokindo@gmail.com
